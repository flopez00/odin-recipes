Deep Dish Pizza

Another staple to the city of Chicago. The best pizza in the country made with a thick and delicious crust

This pizza is often considered the best style of Pizza. This is espcially true among Chicagoans. This pizza has a thick and buttery crust. It's toppings are basically placed in reverse order with the sauce being the top layer and the topping being the bottom layer. in between you have a thich layer of cheese. Yum!

How I order:

Usually just sausage and pepperoni. Can't go wrong there!

Ingredients

  • Deep dish style or any other thick crust
  • your favorite toppings of meats and veggies
  • pizza sauce
  • Provolone and Mozzarella cheeses
  • Butter
  • A side of Garlic bread sticks!
