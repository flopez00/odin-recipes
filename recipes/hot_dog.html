Chicago Style Hot Dogs

The Chicago style Hot Dog is arguably the best there is.

This Hot Dog includes mustard, sport peppers, relish, a pickle, onions and tomato

How I order:

I must admit, sometimes I add ketchup. i know what youre thinking...Ketchup on a Hot Dog!?!? Dont judge me lol

Ingredients

  • Beef dogs
  • Sport peppers
  • Mustard
  • Pickle relish
  • Butter
  • Poppy seed buns
  • Onions, diced
  • Tomato
  • Whole pickles

Steps and Instructions:

  • 1. Boil the beef dogs
  • 2. Srteam the buns
  • 3. On each hot dog bun add: mustard(can be the last step also)
  • 4. Add the beef dog
  • 5. Add the relish and celery salt
  • 6. Add the onions and tomato half moons
  • 7. lastly, add a pickle
